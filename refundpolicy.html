Refund & Returns Policy

Khushi Astrologer provides digital services and courses such as astrology and tarot learning programs, live sessions, and downloadable resources, which are generally non-returnable once accessed or delivered.

Eligibility for Refund

Eligibility window: Refund requests must be raised within 7 days of purchase if no live session was attended, no certificate issued, and less than 10% of course content was consumed.

Ineligible Cases

Non-refundable scenarios: Completed live consultations, attended workshops, issued certificates, or downloaded materials are not eligible for a refund.

Quality & Service Issues

Our commitment: If a paid class is canceled by us or content access fails due to our systems and cannot be restored within 72 hours, a full refund or reschedule will be offered.

Refund Process

How to request: Email support@khushiastrologer.com with your order ID, proof of payment, and a clear reason for the request. If approved, refunds are processed to the original payment method within 7–10 business days.

Rescheduling Policy

1:1 Sessions: A one-time reschedule is allowed if requested at least 12 hours before a scheduled 1:1 session. No-shows are considered services rendered and are non-refundable.
