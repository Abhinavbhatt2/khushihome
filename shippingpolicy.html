Shipping Policy

Most of our offerings are digital. This policy clarifies delivery timelines for digital products and the handling of any physical items.

Digital delivery: Course access credentials and purchase confirmations are sent instantly via email after a successful payment. In rare cases, please allow up to 2 hours for the email to arrive. If you haven't received it, please check your spam or promotions folder before contacting support.

Live sessions: Your booking confirmation, which includes the date, time, and session joining link, is delivered via email and/or WhatsApp. We also send reminders 24 hours and 1 hour before your scheduled session to ensure you don't miss it.

Physical shipments (if applicable): For items like books, decks, or merchandise, orders are dispatched within 2–4 business days. Typical delivery within India takes 3–7 business days via a standard courier service. A tracking ID will be shared with you by email or SMS once the order is shipped.

Delivery issues: Issues such as an incorrect address provided by the customer, missed delivery attempts, or courier delays are outside our direct control. However, we will provide full assistance to help you coordinate with the carrier and resolve the issue.

Customs/fees: For international shipments, buyers are solely responsible for any customs duties, import taxes, or brokerage fees that may be applied by their country's authorities.

Non-delivery: If you do not receive your course access email or session links within 24 hours of purchase, please contact us at contact@khushiastrologer.com for immediate resolution.
